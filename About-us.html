About Us.

We we are
We are a group of concerned Nigerians dedicated to establishing a platform where our political representatives genuinely reflect our interests.

  • Non-Violence At ProbeNaija, we promote and support active democratic engagement that rejects violence.
    We firmly believe that no revolution, democratic struggle, agitation, protest, or disagreement is worth the loss of a single human life. With the intelligence and knowledge bestowed upon us by our Creator, we can establish a people-centered democracy without bloodshed or the destruction of our limited resources.
    We hold the conviction that every human life is unique and precious, and we vehemently oppose all forms of violence in our pursuit of a better Nigerian democracy. Our mission at ProbeNaija is to educate, expose, and empower our fellow Nigerians to unite and reclaim this nation so that its abundant resources benefit all citizens.
    We value the lives of every Nigerian, including those of our corrupt politicians. Our goal is to ensure that our political representatives truly represent us and work for the people. We are committed to achieving this without resorting to any form of violence. As a people, we possess the power to effect significant changes through our sheer numbers, inherent intelligence, and wisdom.
    Let us reject violence towards anyone as we endeavor to reclaim our country from corrupt political leaders.
